This project my own small contribution to Black Lives Matter and in response to the murder of George Floyd—and an effort to further my own education and growth. I wanted to read Dr. King’s letter, but couldn't find it represented well online (other than in PDF form). So I made this site.

Should you be so moved, you can find places to donate to bail funds, learn more about the Black Visions Collective, or search through Florida Elago’s extensive database for ways you can help and/or contribute.

The full source of this project is publically available on GitHub. Please feel free to contribute, or contact me with suggestions or comments.
